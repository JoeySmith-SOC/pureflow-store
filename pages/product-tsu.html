💡 TSU vs RO — Which is right for you?

The TSU removes heavy metals, chlorine, and bacteria while keeping essential minerals. An RO system removes everything including TDS, PFAS, and nitrates. If your main concern is lead, chlorine, or taste — the TSU is perfect. If you want the absolute purest water or have high TDS, go RO.
